GAME INFORMATION & RULES

Objective

The objective of MineSweeper is to clear all the mines from the game board without detonating any of them. The game board consists of a grid of cells, each of which can either contain a mine or be safe. Your goal is to identify the locations of the mines. The objective is to mark all the cells that contain mines and clear all the safe cells without detonating any mines.

Scoring

Your score in Minesweeper is based on the time taken to complete a level, the number of mines cleared will appear all at the bottom of the page. The faster you complete a level, the higher your score will be..

Conclusion

Minesweeper is a classic computer game that has been around since the early 1990s. The game is played on a square grid, where each square represents either a mine or a safe space. The goal of the game is to clear all the safe spaces without detonating any of the hidden mines.
Overall, Minesweeper has become a beloved classic in the world of computer gaming. Despite its simplicity, the game continues to capture the imagination of players around the world.
